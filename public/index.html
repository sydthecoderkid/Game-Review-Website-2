Who I am

Hi! I'm Sydney. I am a programmer and a freshman at Amherst College.

I am massive fan of all things video games and I hope to one day become a gameplay programmer. I have mainly programmed apps and games, but nothing in web development.

So in an effort to diversify my portfolio, I decided it would be a good idea to build my own website from scratch!

I'll just be posting reviews of games I've played so they don't get stuck in my notes app for all eternity.

How I built the website

I used a combination of nodejs, regular javascript, html, and css. Front-end isn't my strong suit though, hence the minimalistic design.

I used mysql to host the information for each game review.

This project was my first forrey into using javascript, and I also really enjoyed spending time working on server-side code.

All in all, this was a fun three week project :)

Let me know if you find any bugs or have any suggestions!
